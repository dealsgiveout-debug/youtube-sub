Privacy Policy

We do not require accounts or logins. When you search for a YouTube channel or paste a YouTube link, the server uses that input to request channel statistics from the YouTube Data API and returns the result to your browser.

We may use cookies and similar technologies if advertisements are enabled (e.g., Google AdSense). Ad providers may use cookies to personalize ads and measure performance.

If you enable AdSense, you should update this page to match your exact configuration and comply with applicable laws (for example, add a cookie notice if required in your region).
